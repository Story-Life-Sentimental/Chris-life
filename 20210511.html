突然就意识到自己做错了呢

Chris的忏悔录

May 12,2021 Chris Chan

We all have some unforgettable experiecnes in our daily life,once upon a time,I had an embarrassing experience.

We had an exam last week,and one of my friends say in front of me at that time.His father told him that if he could get good grades,he would get a new phone that only belongs to him.

When I was writing,he passed me a piece of paper and told me that he wanted to get good grades,he hoped that I could help him cheat,looking at me hopefully.

I refused,but he didn't give up,he started to say dirty words to me.

Finally,I couldn't fucking stand,so I helped him.

I never thought that decision would be my worst decision.

To my surprise,he got better grades than me.When my teacher asked me why I did worse than him,I shouted,"He was such an idiot!Did you REALLY know him?He was a fucking cheater!"

My teacher didn't believe me,my classmates started to laugh,they thought I was the idiot who was crazy about good grades.

……

I made he who he is today.

Since then,he started to like cheating in the exam.

Because of this,he didn't work hard any longer.

I thought he could enter a good school and get a good education.

But I changed that,I made him lazy,I made him work no longer hard.

He has fallen into the abyss.

And the person who threw him into the abyss,I think,it's me.
